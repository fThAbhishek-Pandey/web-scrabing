OVERVIEW
True to its Hindi literal, KOSHISH, a social initiative of REC Ambedkar Nagar students,is mainly focused on providing education to the marginalized section students, at least up to the level where they too can dream big. In KOSHISH, we try to inspire the students to dream and enable them to achieve it through education with the belief that ' simple effort can fill colors in many lives.
